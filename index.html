Résumé

My areas of research are Human-Robot Interaction and Cognitive Architectures. My current research focuses on developing techniques for Rosie, an intelligent agent developed in Soar that dynamically learns new tasks, so that humans can effectively and efficiently discover and then employ its capabilities through natural interaction. The eventual goal is to have Rosie develop mental models of the users in order to customize its interactions to its knowledge about each individual user.

I am also the co-chair of Ensemble of CSE Ladies (ECSEL) at the University of Michigan.
